Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you register, we collect your name and email address. When you use the app, we store the expense records you create — including amounts, categories, dates, and any descriptions you provide. We do not collect payment information or government-issued identification.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide and improve the Spendly service. This includes displaying your expense history, generating summaries, and personalising your experience. We do not use your data for advertising or sell it to any third party.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secured servers. We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all associated data is permanently removed from our systems within 30 days. We recommend exporting your records before deletion if you need them for your own records.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly does not share your personal data with third-party services. We may use infrastructure providers (such as hosting services) to operate the platform, but these providers are contractually prohibited from accessing or using your data for any other purpose.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us. We are committed to addressing any privacy-related concerns promptly and transparently.

Your privacy matters to us. We will never misuse the trust you place in Spendly.
